Why Choose a Hand-Coded Website Instead of a Site Builder?

Site builders like Wix, Squarespace and Shopify make it easy to get something online quickly—but “easy” often comes with tradeoffs in performance, flexibility, and long-term quality. A hand-coded website is built specifically for your business, with clean HTML, purposeful design decisions, and no extra bloat hiding under the hood.

Blazing Fast Performance

Site builders load a lot of code you never see—extra scripts, plugins, and layout engines that slow everything down. A hand-coded site is lean and focused, so it feels fast on real devices, especially on mobile connections.

Built for *Your* Business

Templates are designed to work “okay” for everyone. Hand-coded sites are designed to work beautifully for you—your content, your layout, your priorities, and your brand. Nothing is forced to fit into a pre-made box.

Better SEO Foundation

Clean, semantic HTML and intentional structure give search engines exactly what they need. No messy nested builders, no duplicated headings, and no surprise markup getting in the way of your ranking potential.

A Clear Difference

Hand-Coded Website

  • Fast, clean, semantic HTML
  • Fully custom design and layout
  • No recurring builder platform fees
  • Better control over SEO and structure
  • Optimized assets and performance
  • Built to be extended and refined over time

Site Builder

  • Heavy, bloated code behind the scenes
  • Template constraints and look-alike designs
  • Ongoing subscription just to stay online
  • Less control over markup and metadata
  • Extra scripts that slow pages down
  • Harder to move if the platform changes
